Gaming Tips

PPSSPP RetroArch Save States TrimUI Smart Pro

Use emulators like PPSSPP or RetroArch to experience retro games on modern hardware. Save states and rewind features make revisiting classic games more accessible than ever.

Devices like the TrimUI Smart Pro let you carry hundreds of retro games in your pocket, with support for NDS, PSP, N64, PS1, and GBA titles.
